## Definition

>  **Euler polynomial**
>
>  The **Euler polynomials** $E_n(x)(n=0,1,2,...)$ are generated by
>  $$
>  \frac{2\mathrm{e}^{xt}}{\mathrm{e}^t+1}=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{t^n}{n!}E_n(x).
>  $$
>  where the LHS is the **[[Gerneration function]]**, the series converges when $|t|<\pi$ for the closest singularities near $t=0$ are $t=\pm\pi\mathrm{i}.$



> **Euler number**
>
> **Euler numbers** are generated by the Taylor expansion of $\text{sech}\frac{t}{2}$
> $$
> \frac{2\mathrm{e}^{t/2}}{\mathrm{e}^{t}+1}=\mathrm{sech}\frac t2=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{(-)^n\mathrm{E}_n}{(2n)!}\Big(\frac t2\Big)^{2n}
> $$
> where
> $$
> \mathbf{E}_{n}=(-)^{n}2^{2n}\mathbf{E}_{2n}\big(\:\frac{1}{2}\big)
> $$
> is called the **Euler number**



## Properties

1. **Recursion formula of Euler numbers**

$$
\mathrm{E}_{0}=1,\quad\sum_{l=0}^{k}(-)^{l}{\binom{2k}{2l}}\mathrm{E}_{\iota}=0\quad(k\geqslant1).
$$

2. **Explicit express of Euler polynomial**

$$
\mathbf{E}_n(x)=\sum_{k=0}^{[n/2]}(-)^k\frac{\mathbb{E}_k}{2^{2k}}\binom n{2k}\left(x-\frac12\right)^{n-2k}
$$

3. **Mean value formula**

$$
\mathrm{E}_n(x+1)+\mathrm{E}_n(x)=2x^n.
$$

4. **Differential**

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}^{p}}{\mathrm{d}x^{p}}\mathrm{E}_{n}(x)=\frac{n!}{(n-p)!}\mathrm{E}_{n-p}(x)
$$

5. **Relation of complementary observables**

$$
\mathrm{E}_{n}(1-x)=(-)^{n}\mathrm{E}_{n}(x).
$$

6. **Addition formula**

$$
\begin{aligned}\sum_{s=1}^{m}(-)^{s}s^{n}&=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{s=1}^{m}(-)^{s}[\mathrm{E}_{n}(s+1)+\mathrm{E}_{n}(s)]\\&=\frac{1}{2}[(-)^{m}\mathrm{E}_{n}(m+1)-\mathrm{E}_{n}(1)].\end{aligned}
$$



## Common value and application

### Common value

$$
\begin{aligned}&\mathbf{E}_{0}=1\text{,E}_{1}=1\text{,E}_{2}=5,E_{3}=61,E_{4}=1385,\\&\mathbf{E}_{5}=50\:521,\:\mathbf{E}_{6}=2\:702\:765,\:\mathbf{E}_{7}=199\:360\:981\:,\\&\mathbf{E}_{8}=19\:391\:512\:145,\:\mathbf{E}_{9}=2\:404\:879\:675\:441.\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}&\mathbf{E}_{0}(x)=1\text{,E}_{1}(x)=x-\frac{1}{2},\:\mathrm{E}_{2}(x)=x(x-1)\:,\\&\mathbf{E}_{3}(x)=\left( x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\left( x^{2}-x-\frac{1}{2}\right),\\&\mathbf{E}_{4}(x)=x(x-1)(x^{2}-x-1),\\&\mathbf{E}_{5}(x)=\left( x-\frac{1}{2}\right)(x^{4}-2x^{3}-x^{2}+2x+1),\\&\mathbf{E}_{6}(x)=x(x-1)(x^{4}-2x^{3}-2x^{2}+3x+3).\end{aligned}
$$

### Application
$$
\mathrm{sec}t\:=\:\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{\mathrm{E}_{n}}{(2n)!}t^{2n},\quad|t|<\pi/2.
$$
